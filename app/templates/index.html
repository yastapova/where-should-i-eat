Where Should I Eat?

Not sure what you'd like to eat? Want to try something new? Just plain indecisive? Look no further! Use the map below to see decide what you're in the mood for.

Mouse over a dish on the left or right to see which types of restaurants would have that item.

Mouse over a restaurant type in the center to see what foods it consists of. Click on a box to select it, and click again to unselect.

When you're done, hit the Search button below to search Yelp for restaurants that fit your criteria, and view the results below. Bon Apetit!

If you want to use your geolocation, please give permission.
